About me

Hi, I’m Sophia, I’m 18 years old and I live in Burgh-Haamstede, a quiet town close to the beach. I’m studying HBO-ICT at HZ University of Applied Sciences in Middelburg. I’ve been into computers for as long as I can remember. When I was younger, I spent hours playing games, changing parts in my laptop or PC, and just messing around to see what would happen. I didn’t always know what I was doing, but I loved figuring things out. As I got older, I started wondering more about how computers actually work and what’s going on “behind the scenes” when you run a program. That curiosity is what made me want to learn programming, and now I get to study it for real.

Hobbies

When I’m not busy with school, I like doing a lot of different things. I listen to music pretty much every day sometimes just in the background, sometimes really paying attention to every little detail in a song. Gaming is one of my favorite ways to relax, and I especially like when a game has a beautiful, meaningful story that really sticks with you. I love thrifting because it feels like a treasure hunt you never know what cool or weird stuff you’re going to find. Baking and cooking are also things I really enjoy, especially when I’m trying out a new recipe. Sometimes it goes perfectly, sometimes not… but it’s always fun. And of course, I watch a lot of movies.
